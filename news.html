Nature Communications: CDIN1-Codanin-1 Complex is an RNA Nuclease

Our work on the CDIN1-Codanin-1 complex — defective in Congenital Dyserythropoietic Anaemia Type I — has been accepted in Nature Communications. This paper reveals a previously unknown RNA nuclease activity with implications for understanding CDA-I disease mechanisms.
